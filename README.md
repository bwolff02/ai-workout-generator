# AI Workout Generator

An intelligent workout program generator that creates personalized training plans based on your experience level, goals, equipment, and preferences.

## Features

- **Intelligent Questionnaire** - 10-step form that learns about your training style
- **Personalized Programs** - Generates custom workout splits based on your answers
- **Wave Periodization** - Alternating heavy/volume weeks for optimal gains
- **Equipment Flexibility** - Works with any gym setup, home equipment, or bodyweight
- **Training Techniques** - Support for supersets, drop sets, rest-pause, and more
- **Volume Targeting** - Evidence-based set recommendations per muscle group
- **Detailed Exercises** - 70+ exercises with form cues and progressions

## How It Works

1. **Answer Questions** - Tell us about your experience, goals, schedule, and preferences
2. **Generate Program** - AI creates a customized training plan instantly
3. **Get Details** - Each exercise includes sets, reps, weight suggestions, and notes
4. **Track Progress** - Log your workouts and track improvements over time

## Getting Started

Visit the [AI Workout Generator](https://bwolff02.github.io/ai-workout-generator/) and click "Generate My Program" to start.

## Built With

- Vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks)
- LocalStorage for data persistence
- Evidence-based training science
- Deterministic generation (reproducible results)

## Features Include

- Full-body, upper/lower, and push/pull/legs splits
- 45-90 minute session customization
- Beginner to advanced experience levels
- Strength, hypertrophy, and recomp goals
- Conditional UI (advanced features only for experienced lifters)
- Provenance tracking (understand why each choice was made)
